1
🏗️
Foundation
The non-negotiable base
1.1 Product-Market Fit
Solving a real problem for a specific audience. Without this, nothing else matters.
1.2 Clear Value Proposition
One sentence that explains why someone should buy from you vs. competitors.
1.3 Target Audience Definition
Specific demographics, psychographics, pain points, and buying triggers.
1.4 Competitive Positioning
Where you fit in the market and your unique angle/differentiation.
Priority Level
Critical — Do not proceed without
2
🎯
Conversion Engine
Turning visitors into customers
2.1 High-Converting Landing Page
Clear headline, compelling copy, social proof, strong CTA, frictionless design.
2.2 Sales Copy & Messaging
Persuasive copy that addresses objections and motivates action.
2.3 Checkout & Payment Flow
Minimal steps, multiple payment options, trust signals, mobile-optimized.
2.4 Offer Structure
Pricing, bundles, guarantees, urgency/scarcity mechanisms.
Priority Level
Critical — Revenue depends on this
3
🚦
Traffic Acquisition
Getting qualified visitors
3.1 Paid Advertising (PPC)
Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads — scalable but requires budget and testing.
3.2 Organic Social Media
Content that builds audience and drives engagement over time.
3.3 SEO & Content Marketing
Long-term traffic through search rankings and valuable content.
3.4 Partnerships & Affiliates
Leveraging other people's audiences through collaborations.
Priority Level
High — Start with 1-2 channels
4
📧
Email & Retention
Maximizing customer lifetime value
4.1 Lead Capture System
Pop-ups, lead magnets, welcome offers to build email list.
4.2 Automated Email Sequences
Welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back campaigns.
4.3 Newsletter & Broadcasts
Regular value-driven communication to stay top-of-mind.
4.4 Loyalty & Subscription
Recurring revenue models, rewards programs, exclusive member benefits.
Priority Level
High — Highest ROI channel
5
⭐
Social Proof & Trust
Reducing friction and building confidence
5.1 Customer Reviews
Product reviews, testimonials, case studies, user-generated content.
5.2 Trust Signals
Security badges, guarantees, clear policies, contact info, about page.
5.3 Influencer Validation
Third-party endorsements, unboxing videos, expert recommendations.
5.4 Community Building
Discord, Facebook groups, forums — engaged customers become advocates.
Priority Level
Medium-High — Builds over time
6
📊
Analytics & Optimization
Data-driven improvement
6.1 Conversion Tracking
Proper analytics setup, pixel implementation, attribution modeling.
6.2 A/B Testing
Continuous testing of headlines, images, CTAs, pricing, offers.
6.3 Funnel Analysis
Identifying drop-off points and optimization opportunities.
6.4 Customer Feedback Loop
Surveys, support tickets, reviews — listen and iterate based on input.
Priority Level
Medium — Start basic, expand
7
🚀
Scale & Expansion
Growing what works
7.1 Ad Budget Scaling
Increase spend on profitable campaigns while maintaining ROAS.
7.2 New Product Lines
Expand catalog based on customer demand and market research.
7.3 New Channels
Add traffic sources once core channels are optimized.
7.4 Operational Systems
Automation, team building, processes that support growth.
Priority Level
Low — Only after 1-6 are working
