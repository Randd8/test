Why Fractional Makes Sense

"Isn't fractional more expensive per ounce?"

Yes. Premiums are higher on smaller denominations. That's true industry-wide.

But here's what that misses:

Accessibility beats perfection. Would you rather own zero ounces because the price felt like too much—or own fractional silver because it felt doable?

Variety has value. Owning different handmade designs is more interesting than identical rounds. Collectability matters. Your level of enjoyment matters.

Fractional is transactional. The rising cost of precious metals is making it harder to trade in high value bars. Fractional allows for the ease and possibility of everyday transactions in silver.

Premiums shrink with volume. Flash sales and bulk discounts bring cost down significantly.

Bottom line: If you're optimizing purely for cost-per-ounce, traditional heavy weight bullion wins. But if you're optimizing for experience, variety, transactability and easily consistent stacking habits—fractional is unbeatable.
