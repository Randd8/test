Why Fractional Makes Sense

"Isn't fractional more expensive per ounce?"

Yes. Premiums are higher on smaller denominations. That's true industry-wide.

But here's what that misses:

Accessibility beats perfection. Would you rather own zero ounces because the price felt like too much—or own real silver because it felt doable?

Variety has value. Owning different handmade designs is more interesting than identical rounds. Collectibility matters. Joy matters.

Fractional is flexible. Need a gift? Grab a gram. Want to test a design? Grab a gram. Scaling up? Grab 20.
